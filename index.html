How it works

Not a job board.
A matching platform.

We pair athletes and clubs on skill, coaching style, personality, housing needs, and culture — not just a résumé and a prayer.

01

Build your profile

Athletes create a verified profile with playing level, coaching experience, availability, references, and a video intro. Clubs post with full context.

02

Get matched

Our platform surfaces the best fits based on sport, skill, personality, housing needs, and culture. No cold applying into the void.

03

Connect & confirm

Clubs review ranked applicants, message athletes directly, and confirm placements — all in one place. No back-and-forth email chains.

04

Build reputation

After the season, both parties review each other. Your reputation score travels with you — building trust that compounds every summer.
